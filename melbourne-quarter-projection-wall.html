Melbourne Quarter Projection Wall

2017

Unsigned Studio was engaged by Squint/Opera to create a custom hardware and software solution for a multi-projector installation.

Bringing together brand assets, films and 360 imagery we created a unique experience. The completed system could be enjoyed during events and has real utility when used in a meeting setting.

Client: Squint/Opera

Direction: Dave Mackenzie

Design: Björn Rüst

Each of the modes could be toggled between and controlled by a custom dial we built. Manufactured from the CNC milled aluminium, 3d printed internals and an open source Arduino microcontroller.

PROJECTION

The four modes included: a branding showcase bringing together imagery and film, a slower ambient mode, a controllable 360 view-line mode and cinema mode.

Branding Mode. Videos and still imagery rotate around the wall.

Ambient Mode. Static videos slowly shifting colour.

Viewline Mode. Full 360 degree view-lines. The heading can be controlled by the dial.
